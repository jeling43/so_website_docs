Judicial Bureau Divisions

Jail Division

The Jail Division is responsible for the safe and secure operation of the county jail and its inmate occupants, and for maintaining the required records of incarcerated persons.

View Jail Division

Court Services Division

The Court Services Division receives and serves all court process, including warrants, civil actions, and summonses; transports inmates; and provides physical security for the Superior Court and judges.
